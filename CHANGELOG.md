## 1.0.0

- Initial release.
- 831 Ant Design icons included: 447 Outlined, 234 Filled, and 150 TwoTone.
- `AntdIcon` widget with `size`, `color`, and `secondaryColor` support.
- Inherits color from Flutter's ambient `IconTheme`.
- TwoTone icons support a configurable secondary color.
- Rendering via `flutter_svg` with inline SVG.
- Icons auto-generated by `tools/generate.dart`.
